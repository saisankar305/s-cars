BMW(Bayerische Motoren Werke AG)

BMW-X3

The BMW X3 is a compact luxury crossover SUV manufactured by German automaker BMW since 2003. Based on the BMW 5 Series platform (G01), and now in its third generation, BMW markets the crossover as a Sports Activity Vehicle, the company's proprietary descriptor for its X-line of vehicles.

BMW-3-SERIES

The BMW 3 Series is a compact executive car manufactured by the German automaker BMW since May 1975. It is the successor to the 02 Series and has been produced in seven different generations..

BMW-i4

The BMW i4 is an all-electric four-door fastback styled sedan under development by German automobile manufacturer BMW, which was first unveiled as a concept car named i Vision Dynamics at the 2017 Frankfurt Motor Show.

BMW-X5

The BMW X5 is a mid-size luxury, rear-wheel drive SUV produced by BMW. The X5 made its debut in 1999. It was BMW's first SUV and it also featured all-wheel drive and was available with either manual or automatic transmission. In 2006, the second generation X5 was launched, known internally as the E70.

The BMW X7 is a full-sized luxury sport utility vehicle manufactured by BMW. It is BMW's flagship Sports Activity Vehicle (SAV).
